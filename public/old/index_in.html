WELCOME TO SANDHOORA HOLDINGS

We are a multi disciplinary general contractor specialized in both commercial and residential construction operating with the vision bringing dreams to life. Our project diversity and commitment to quality has allowed us to exceed client needs and expectations within the short span of our service.

ABOUT US

Sandhoora Holdings is a multi disciplinary Construction Company in Sri Lanka delivering high quality buildings and infrastructure, catering to commercial and domestic sectors. At Sandhoora, we possess a wealth of diverse experience and exposure to execute all types of building constructions. Spearheaded by a veteran in the industry with over 30 years experience and successful track record, Sandhoora is prepared to provide end to end solutions to complex designs and deliver high quality end products to its patrons.

Qualified and experienced management and technical teams of the Company are assisted by a separate team of skilled and competent group of workers. The core values of Sandhoora is to provide the clients Creativity, Quality, Reliability service and most importantly Value for Money every single client.
